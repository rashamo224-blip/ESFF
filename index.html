02 — Fund Strategy

Investment Thesis & Approach

The Equivator Saudi FinTech Fund targets high-growth Near-Series A and Series A fintech companies in Saudi Arabia, with selective GCC exposure. We operate as a PE-discipline Fixer — not a passive minority investor.

Every investment comes with dedicated operational professionals (CFO, legal, BD) deployed directly into portfolio companies. Our 30/60/90-day intervention framework accelerates profitability, governance, and exit readiness within months — not years.

The fund takes 15–25% average ownership through a hybrid equity and debt structure, targeting an IRR of 18–20% and a 3× MOIC over an 8-year term.
